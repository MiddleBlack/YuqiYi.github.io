HOW LONG WILL YOU LIVE

During the long history, many emperors and generals are persistent in the pursuit of immortality. For example, the pyramid of Egypt, one of the seven wonders of the world, is a luxurious mausoleum prepared by Khufu for the immortality of himself and his wife. After the first emperor of Qin completed the great cause of unifying China, he also began to seek immortality and dream of that. Although ordinary people do not have the supreme rights and status, people will instinctively pursue the length of life. Longevity is the common dream of the people from all ages. So in this world, people gradually begin to care about how long their life span is regarded as longevity, and more importantly, how long they can live. However, can you accurately estimate your life span? The answer, obviously, is No. Because there are too many factors affecting life. Heredity, geography, gender, stress, income, lifestyle, physical condition and medical resources, as well as luck all affect life expectancy. Life expectancy can not be accurately estimated, but the distribution of life expectancy has its own rules. Most people's life expectancy is close to the average life expectancy, as the result, as members of the society, we can approximately estimate individual life expectancy by the average life expectancy. Meanwhile, life expectancy is an important indicator to measure the level of health and an important reference to enjoy life.
